Progress Isn't Always Visible

Jan 1, 2007

Sometimes the most significant progress happens beneath the surface.

Progress does not annouce itself right away most of the time its quiet like habits changing without you knowing

Its easy to fill stuck when theres nothing obvious to point at, no milestones, no finish line, no results, and nothing in the moment saying "This is working", although you cant see it something is changing even if you cant see it does not mean nothing is happening some things just need more time to be noticeable and seen.

Growth is growth even if you cant see even if its slow it is shaping us in what comes next and from time to time understanding it is progress on its own.
