Learning To Be Okay With Silence

Jan 19, 2005

Thoughts on embracing silence and finding peace within it.

Silence is often avoided as we fill it with noise, conversations, notifs, or background noises treating it as if that silence is uncomfortable by default yet silence isn't really a problem but rather what comes when everything falls silent.

When it falls silent your thought surfaces more clearly it may be overwhelming especially when those thoughts are unresolved or not familliar to you its easy to distract ourselves than to sit with thoughts when its silent, silence ask for attention even if you dont want to give it some and its uncomfortable at first.

Its okay to be with silence even if your thoughts surfaces more clearly some of them does not require any meaning or answers their simply asking for attention simply asking for a moment of silence and peace where nothing happens theres a certain peace within it that doesn't require action it can exist without it being filled, explained or fixed and in those moments you may know more about yourself than ever.
