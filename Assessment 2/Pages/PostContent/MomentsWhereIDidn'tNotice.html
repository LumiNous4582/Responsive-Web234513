Moments Where I Didn't Notice

April 6, 2004

A Reflection on easily missed experience.

Some moments pass without asking for your attention they dont announce themselves or feel important at the time it's only later when they resurface again, small details, expressions or choices that is now set in stone.

Were so focused on what comes next the mind moving faster than its present moment to the point were skipping over things that dont seem urgent at the time and in doing so we miss a few small yet important things not because they weren't important but rather people failing to notice them yet.

Looking back those moments that seem small at the time become bigger reminding us that not everything announces its importance as some stay quiet and waiting patiently for us to slow down and look back to see them

Cherised every moment
