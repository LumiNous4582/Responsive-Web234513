Time Passes Even When Nothing Happens

Feb 5, 2004

A Short Reflection on how time just moves, even without being told to.

There are always days when nothing feels memorable nothing major, no event, no progress and no moments that stood out enough to be remember later they just pass by quietly blending with days similar to each other.

Time does not slow down during these moments even when it feels still and slow time is still moving at the exact same time without hesitation

Its easy to believe that time only matters when something is happening, if change is visible and growth is measured but everything matters even the smaller moments whether the days are uneventful or not, maybe their not empty at all and just hard to notice and perhaps thats enough.
