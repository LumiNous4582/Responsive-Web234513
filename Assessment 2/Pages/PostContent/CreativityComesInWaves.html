Creativity Comes In Waves

Jan 13, 2005

A reflection on the flow of creativity and harnessing it when it comes.

Creativity rarely arrives on our whims or commands days past by without ideas flowing easily, where our thoughts connect naturally and where everything feels effortless to do. Then there are days where nothing happens a string of silence stretched throughout the day which then leads to forcing creativity to come which only results into you frustration.

For quite some time i treated thosse quiet days as failures as i failed in forcing creativity assumptions lead to me losing motivation or just falling behind before finally realising that creativity just simply moves at its own pace like how waves comes and go.

I learned to respect how it comes and go and adjusted how i worked as instead of just forcing creativity i observe, collect ideas, rest and repeat as that way maybe it'll come faster and when it arrives i give it a room to exist as it wont last long but its fine as waiting again for it to come back again is part of the process.
