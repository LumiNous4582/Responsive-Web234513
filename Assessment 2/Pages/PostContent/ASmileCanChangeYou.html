A Smile Can Change You

June 12, 2021

A reflection on the power of a smile and its impact on our lives.

I used to think a smile only mattered when something good happens that it was just a response and not a choice but unexpectedly just smiling despite not being happy can sometimes change how it unfolds.

Its not dramatic or instant change as nothing became perfect by just smiling, yet by just smiling changes me little by little i never noticed until everything started to become lighter, thoughts less stress, time still feels slow but right, conversations feels more better it made me realised a small smile can change anything even if theres nothing good in the world.

Im not suggesting that a singular smile can fixed everything nor that it could replaced its genuine emotions we feel, we'll all encounter heavy, and silent days and thats fine just smiling you have them can be an act of kindness from you and to others, so when the world pushes you down and drop you get up and SMILE.
